### 1. Attention U-Net as a surrogate model for groundwater prediction

- **Authors:** Maria Luisa Taccari, Johannes Nuttall, Xuan Chen, Sami El Moctar, John Pollington
- **Year:** 2022
- **Journal/Source:** Advances in Water Resources
- **DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.advwatres.2022.104169
